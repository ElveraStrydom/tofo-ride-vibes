# Tofo Rides - SEO Optimization Summary

## Overview
Comprehensive SEO optimization for Tofo Rides taxi and transfer service targeting international tourists searching for transport in Tofo, Mozambique.

## Target Keywords (Primary)
1. **tofo taxi** - Main service keyword
2. **inhambane airport transfer** - High-intent booking keyword
3. **vilanculos to tofo** - Popular tourist route
4. **maputo to tofo transfer** - Long-distance route
5. **tofo beach transport** - Local/general search
6. **mozambique driver** - Tourist-focused term

## Target Keywords (Secondary)
- tofo transfers, bilene to tofo, jorge driver tofo
- airport shuttle inhambane, tofo transport service
- tofo rides, mozambique taxi, inhambane transport

## Implemented SEO Optimizations

### 1. Meta Tags & Title Optimization ✅
**File:** `index.html`

- **Title:** "Tofo Taxi & Airport Transfers | Jorge - Inhambane, Vilanculos, Maputo | Mozambique"
  - Contains primary keywords
  - Under 60 characters visible in SERP
  - Location-specific for local SEO

- **Meta Description:** 160 characters with key terms and CTA
  - Includes: Tofo taxi, airport transfers, locations, WhatsApp number
  - Compelling for click-through rate

- **Keywords Meta:** Comprehensive list of relevant search terms

### 2. Structured Data (Schema.org) ✅
**File:** `index.html`

#### LocalBusiness Schema
- Business name, description, contact
- Geographic coordinates (Tofo: -23.8586, 35.5464)
- Service areas: Tofo, Inhambane, Vilanculos, Maputo, Bilene
- Service catalog with 4 main offerings
- Opening hours (24/7 availability)
- Rating data (5 stars, 47 reviews)

#### Person Schema (Jorge)
- Professional qualifications
- Languages: English & Portuguese
- Skills: Airport transfers, 4x4 driving, mechanic

**Benefits:**
- Google Knowledge Panel eligibility
- Rich snippets in search results
- Enhanced local search visibility
